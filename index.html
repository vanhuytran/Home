How BMI work

BMI is a body mass index calculator. Your BMI is calculated by dividing your body weight in kilograms by your height in metres squared (m2). It's a useful working guide for most adults over 18 and can tell you a lot. The Body Mass Index (BMI) is a statistical measure of body weight based on a person's weight and height.
